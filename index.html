Understanding the Equation: y = mx + b

m (Slope): Determines how steep the line is. Positive = upward, Negative = downward, Zero = horizontal.

b (Y-Intercept): Where the line crosses the y-axis (vertical axis).

Current line: For every 1 unit right, the line goes 1 unit up, starting at y = 0.
